Volunteer

Louisville Books to Prisoners is an all-volunteer organization. We need volunteers to help perform the following tasks: Sorting, picking and packing books Contacting additional facilities to obtain authorization to ship Managing our social media (Facebook and soon Instagram) Fundraising to meet 2019 goals: 50% increase in number of inmates reached

Donate Funds

We are a 501c3 organization and your donations are tax deductible. With anticipated requests in 2019, our work is only made possible by donors like you. We are 100% volunteer driven and our only significant operational costs are postage and packing materials. It costs, on average, $1.50 to send one book directly to a prisoner in our service area

Donate Books

We have crates at McQuixote Books and Coffee for drop off donations and are in need of the following types of books: Art & Drawing Instructional Manuals Home Repair / Trade Skills Current Computer Skills Music & Guitar Tabs Language Instruction (German, Spanish, etc.) Race Topics Feminist Topics LGBT+ Topics Current Law & Litigation Books Dictionaries
